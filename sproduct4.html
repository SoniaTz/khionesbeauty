Product Details
For those who loved Relief Sun but found it a bit too rich, Beauty of Joseon introduces Relief Sun: Aqua-Fresh.
This new formula is made with rice seed water and panthenol to deliver refreshing hydration while protecting

Relief Sun Aqua-Fresh offers the same benefits as the original Relief Sun—no white cast, no sticky feel

Hydrating & Soothing UV Defense:
Infused with nutrient-rich rice seed water and soothing panthenol, this sunscreen not only protects but also revitalizes
skin stressed from sun exposure and heat.

Fast-Absorbing Lotion Texture:
Like the original Relief Sun, Aqua-Fresh has a watery, quick-absorbing consistency that blends seamlessly into
the skin without leaving white residue or greasiness. The lightweight formula ensures a fresh finish
—even with multiple layers.

Texture:
This sunscreen features a hydrating lotion-like texture that glides on smoothly,
leaving your skin feeling fresh and comfortable with no white or blue undertones.

Targets:Hydration, Protection
Suitable for:All Skin Types
Key ingredients:
Rice Seed Water (30%)
A respected beauty ingredient, rice is packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants that help restore
the skin’s natural health, leaving it clear and radiant.
Panthenol (Vitamin B5)
Known for its excellent moisturizing and soothing properties, panthenol strengthens the skin’s
moisture barrier and calms skin irritated by UV rays and environmental aggressors.

Experience the perfect balance of hydration and protection with Relief Sun Aqua-Fresh, designed for those
